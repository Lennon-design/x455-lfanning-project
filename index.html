Custom Bundles

These custom bundles are pieced together to create hand-tailored outfits. Each bundle is unique and priced differently depending on which pieces are in the bundle.

Bundle 1 - $200

Bundle 2 - $250

Bundle 3 - $300


Bundles are not personalizable, each bundle is already established. New bundles will be released at the beginning of the month, be sure to check back in to see what new bundles are created. Each bundle will come with a card explaining the selected outfit: where the inspiration for the outfit came from, what the stylistic sensibilities for the bundle are, and a suggested way to wear the outfit. To see general inspiration for outfit direciton visit our are.na page and our depop page

Featured Picks Details

These featured items are specially chosen each month as favorited samples from our latest collection. These specially chosen items rotate every month. These pieces are purchaseable just like any other item however come at a premium as these pieces have been highlighted for either being rare, valuable, or hot items. This month's picks were found in the East Bay of the San Francisco Bay area, in pre-loved shops: some big, some smaller, all these pieces are vintage, of course.
